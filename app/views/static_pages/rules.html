Basic competition rules:
1. Bracket consists of four stages:

A. GROUPS STAGE: Every competitor should initially complete their forecasts for the group stages before the tournament kick-off (forecasts will be disabled after that moment). Points will be assigned according to the rules defined next. Once the groups stage is complete, quarter finals stage will start.

B. QUARTER FINALS STAGE:

Once the quarter finalists are defined, each competitor will be able to forecast the result for each of the quarter final matches. Again, quarter finals forecasts will be disabled after quarter finals kick-off.

B. SEMI FINALS STAGE:

Once the semi finalists are defined, each competitor will be able to forecast the result for each of the semi final matches. Again, semi finals forecasts will be disabled after semi finals kick-off.

B. FINALS STAGE:

Once the tournament finalists are defined, each competitor will be able to forecast the result for final match as well as the third-place match. Again, finals forecasts will be disabled after third-place match kick-off.

2. Brackets points will be awarded as follows:

A. GROUPS STAGE MATCHES:
Each groups stage's match will award competitors points according to these rules:
3 POINTS: Correctly forecasted match's winner team or draw  
1 POINT: Correctly forecasted number of goals scored by a team in the match  

E.g: Match ended: TEAM A 2 - TEAM B 3   
Competitor forecasted: TEAM A 0 - TEAM B 3: (Earns 3 + 0 + 1 = 4 points) 
Competitor forecasted: TEAM A 2 - TEAM B 0: (Earns 0 + 1 + 0 = 1 point) 
Competitor forecasted: TEAM A 3 - TEAM B 3: (Earns 0 + 0 + 1 = 1 point) 
Competitor forecasted: TEAM A 2 - TEAM B 3: (Earns 3 + 1 + 1 = 5 points)
